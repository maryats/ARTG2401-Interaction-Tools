Husky T-Shirt Design
For a contest at Northeastern University.

About

I designed this t-shirt graphic for a contest held by my dorm complex at Northeastern University. The group of dormitories is often referred to as the “S.S. Huntington” complex, hence the idea to incorporate a life-preserver into the design.

Process

My design was chosen and printed for the residents of the dorm complex. This was my second time ever designing a t-shirt.
